About FRBEAMS

Empowering Jinka University with smart attendance solutions

Revolutionizing Attendance Management

FRBEAMS is a cutting-edge attendance management system specifically designed for Jinka University. Our facial recognition technology eliminates the need for traditional attendance methods, providing a seamless, accurate, and efficient solution.

Located in Jinka, Ethiopia, our university embraces innovation and technology to enhance administrative processes. FRBEAMS represents our commitment to modernizing educational infrastructure while maintaining the highest standards of security and privacy.
